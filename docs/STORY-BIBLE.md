# ForgeCity story bible (outline)

Tone: hopeful reconstruction after a Digital Collapse. You are the Architect.
The home screen *is* the map.

## Chapter 1 — Embers (MVP)

City mostly dark. Tutorial quests:

1. Raise the first twenty buildings (install/launchable apps placed on the grid).
2. Restore the first power grid (night lighting unlock).

Reward: Forge District fully lit; scrap income begins.

## Chapter 2 — Foundations

UsageStats-backed focus quests. Timed deep-work sessions unlock taller buildings
and open Vault / Nexus / Archive.

## Chapter 3 — Specialization

Branching paths:

- **Vault Empire** — heavy trading/finance usage → gold aesthetics, market widgets.
- **Tech Spire** — heavy coding/notes usage → terminal overlays, denser Forge towers.
- Hybrid balance keeps both districts healthy.

## Chapter 4 — Rivals

Distraction-heavy apps become temporary obstacle districts. Guardian Agent
(planned) can dim Arena during focus quests.

## Chapter 5 — Symphony

Ending variants from long-term balance metrics. Full city lights for balanced
Architects; path endings for Vault- or Forge-dominant histories.

## Side quests (templates)

| Template | Trigger | Reward |
|----------|---------|--------|
| Clean the Archive | Many downloads / gallery opens | Archive district tier+ |
| Silent Nexus | Reduce late-night messaging | Nexus calm aura |
| Vault Vigil | Complete a paper-trading streak | Gold dust |

AI Story Weaver (Phase 4) adapts titles/copy from local usage summaries; never
exfiltrates raw notification content without explicit user opt-in.
